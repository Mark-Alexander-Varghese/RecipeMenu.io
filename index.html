Dragon Chicken

Ingredients

    For Marination:

  • 500g Boneless Chicken(cut into strips)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tanlespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon red chilli sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1/4 cup cornflour
  • 1 egg

    • For Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped garlic
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped ginger
  • 1 onion sliced
  • 3-4 green chillies
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons red chilli sauce
  • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar

Instructions

    Marinate the Chicken

  • In a large bowl, mix chicken strips with ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, red chili sauce, salt, black pepper, cornflour, all-purpose flour, and egg. Let it marinate for 15–20 minutes.

    • Fry the Chicken

  • Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add marinated chicken strips and fry until golden and crispy. Remove and set aside.

    • Prepare the Sauce

  • In a separate pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil. Add chopped garlic and ginger; sauté until golden brown. Add sliced onions, bell peppers, and green chilies. Stir-fry on high heat for 2–3 minutes.

    • Combine Ingredients

  • Add soy sauce, red chili sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, and sugar. Mix well. Add fried chicken strips to the pan and toss to coat with the sauce.

    • Final touch

  • Adjust salt to taste. Garnish with chopped spring onions and roasted cashews.
